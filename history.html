History of Holograms

Holography began with Dennis Gabor's 1947 invention, but practical holograms emerged in the 1960s with laser technology. Early holograms were static, but advancements led to dynamic displays in the 1980s. Today, holograms power augmented reality (AR) and virtual reality (VR) experiences.

Key milestones include the first laser hologram in 1962 and holographic movies in the 1970s.
