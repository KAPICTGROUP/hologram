Modern Applications

Holograms are used in diverse fields: in medicine for 3D imaging of organs, in entertainment for concerts and gaming, and in security for anti-counterfeiting measures like holographic IDs. Emerging uses include AR glasses for navigation and holographic teleconferencing.
